About Instagram

Instagram is a social networking service owned by Meta Platforms, previously Facebook. It originated from Burbn, a check-in mobile app developed by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger in San Francisco. Users can upload and share photos and videos, which can be edited within the app. Available on iOS and Android, Instagram enables users to share moments with followers, using hashtags and geotags for discoverability.
